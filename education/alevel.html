Exam Tips

Practical support for staying organised and handling higher-level study demands.

  • Break subjects down into topic lists and track weak areas honestly
  • Use mark schemes and examiner reports, not just revision notes
  • Prioritise essay planning and timed answers for writing-heavy subjects
  • Keep returning to old topics so revision stays cumulative
  • Build routines that include sleep, breaks and time away from study
