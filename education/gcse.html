Exam Tips

Practical steps that help keep revision steady and exam days calmer.

  • Break large subjects into smaller topic lists
  • Use past papers to spot patterns in question styles
  • Focus on understanding before memorising quotes or formulas
  • Build a revision routine you can actually keep up with
  • Leave time for rest, meals and sleep during exam season
