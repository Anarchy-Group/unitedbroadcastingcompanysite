Tips & Revision Checklist

Keep your coursework organised and tick off areas as you develop them.

  • Show the journey of your ideas, not just the finished result
  • Experiment widely so your project feels thoughtful and personal
  • Use short, clear annotations to explain your choices
  • Look closely at artists and explain how they influence your work
  • Make sure your final piece clearly connects to your earlier development
