Tips & Revision Checklist

Keep revision simple and tick off areas as you cover them.

  • Always apply your answer to the business in the question or case study
  • Use business vocabulary accurately, not just general opinions
  • Practise short calculations until they feel quick and confident
  • When evaluating, explain why one option may be better than another
  • Read command words carefully so you know whether to explain, analyse or evaluate
