Tips & Revision Checklist

Keep revision simple and tick off areas as you cover them.

  • Learn key definitions carefully because chemistry mark schemes depend on precise wording
  • Practise balancing equations regularly so it becomes automatic
  • Show every step in calculations so you can still earn method marks
  • Use the periodic table actively during revision, not just for reference
  • Link required practicals to the topic they support so the content feels more connected
