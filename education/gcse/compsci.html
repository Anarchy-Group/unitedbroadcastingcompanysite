Tips & Revision Checklist

Keep revision simple and tick off areas as you cover them.

  • Practise both theory and programming regularly so one doesn’t get left behind
  • Learn the meaning of key technical terms carefully
  • Use binary and logic exercises little and often so they become easier over time
  • When coding, test small parts first instead of trying to fix everything at once
  • Revise examples of cyber security threats and protections together
