Tips & Revision Checklist

Keep your theory and coursework organised and tick off areas as you cover them.

  • Always link your design choices back to the user and the design brief
  • Use clear annotations so your thinking is easy to follow
  • Show development, not just a final idea, in your project work
  • Learn the strengths and weaknesses of different materials and processes
  • Evaluate honestly so improvements feel realistic and thoughtful
