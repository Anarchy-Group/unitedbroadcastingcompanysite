Tips & Revision Checklist

Keep your practical and written work organised and tick off areas as you cover them.

  • Always make your performance choices clear and purposeful
  • Use drama terminology accurately when writing about performance
  • Explain how voice, movement and staging affect the audience
  • Practise short extracts often so confidence builds naturally
  • When evaluating, be honest about what worked and what could improve
