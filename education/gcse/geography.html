Tips & Revision Checklist

Keep revision simple and tick off areas as you cover them.

  • Learn case studies well enough to use specific detail, not just general points
  • Practise map, graph and data skills little and often
  • Use geography keywords accurately in every answer
  • Make sure you can explain processes step by step, especially in physical geography
  • For longer questions, build points with evidence and explanation clearly
