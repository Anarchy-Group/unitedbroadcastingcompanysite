Tips & Revision Checklist

Keep revision simple and tick off areas as you cover them.

  • Revise vocabulary little and often instead of all at once
  • Practise speaking out loud so pronunciation becomes more natural
  • Use at least two time frames in writing to sound more developed
  • Learn useful opinion phrases you can use in many topics
  • Read questions carefully so you answer in the right tense and context
