Tips & Revision Checklist

Keep revision simple and tick off areas as you cover them.

  • Always read the question carefully before starting your answer
  • Use short quotations to support your points clearly
  • Explain the effect of a method, not just name it
  • Plan your writing so your ideas stay focused and organised
  • Practise under timed conditions so the real exam feels more familiar
