Tips & Revision Checklist

Keep revision simple and tick off areas as you cover them.

  • Always connect your ideas back to the question being asked
  • Use short quotations and explain them clearly
  • Revise themes and characters together so links feel natural
  • Practise planning essays before writing full answers
  • Learn a few strong quotations really well instead of too many weak ones
