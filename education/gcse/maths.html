Tips & Revision Checklist

Keep revision simple and tick off areas as you cover them.

  • Always show your working out, even if the answer seems obvious
  • Check whether your final answer is sensible
  • Practise different question styles, not just the easy ones
  • Revisit weak topics regularly instead of leaving them too long
  • Use past papers to get familiar with timing and structure
