Practical Tips

Simple guidance for building steady confidence and useful everyday habits.

  • Keep a record of achievements, tasks and experiences you can use later
  • Practise speaking about yourself clearly and confidently
  • Build digital habits that are organised, safe and professional
  • Use small weekly goals instead of trying to improve everything at once
  • Ask for feedback and learn how to use it positively
